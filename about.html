Our Story

Cache Cow Snow Plows was founded with a simple mission: to help our neighbors in Logan survive winter. What started as a single pickup truck with a plow has grown into a fleet of "cows" (our affectionately named trucks) roaming the streets of Cache Valley.

We believe in reliability, transparency, and a bit of local humor. Winter is tough, but your snow removal service shouldn't be.
